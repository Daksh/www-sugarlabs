GETTING STARTED

Using the Frame

Take the mouse to one of the corners, in order to reveal the frame.
In order to change the settings and sensitivity of revealing the frame, kindly check out the next section.

Once the frame is revealed, you will see a lot of icons in it. Amongst the top, you will find the options to 1. Go to Neighborhood View (F1) 2. Group View (F2) 3. Home (F3) 4. Activity (F4) 5. Journal (F5) You can also find a lot of other options in the frame. You can explore these by clicking the respective icons.

Changing Frame Settings

On home, click the XO Icon, and choose My Settings.

In the Settings Window, select Frame Icon.

In the Activation Delay, you can choose the delay it takes to show the frame from the edges as well as corners.

Using Journal

From the frame, Click on the Journal button.

This will open up the Journal.
You can think of this as call history, but a much more sophisticated, usable and powerful. It logs all the times you open an activity and what you do inside it. You can click on any instance and add/view/edit the description.

Connect to a Wi-Fi

Take the mouse to one of the corners, in order to reveal the frame.

Click on the Neighborhood button from the frame.

Search for the Wi-Fi SSID from amongst all the circles shown.
Click on the Wi-Fi and select Connect.

If it is the first time use, it will ask for the Wi-Fi password.
Entering that will connect you to the Wi-Fi successfully.

Collaboration

After connecting to the Wi-Fi, go to the Neighborhood view. Here you will find a huge number of XO icons on the screen. You can add the people you want to collaborate with, as friends from this screen.

Now to share with the help of collaboration, go to the Journal and click the activity instance you want to share. In Send To, you will find the people you can collaborate with. NOTE: This feature is currently unavailable in sugar-build.
